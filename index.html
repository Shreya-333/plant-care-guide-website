Plant Care Tips 🌞

💧 Watering
Water only when the topsoil feels dry. Overwatering is the most common mistake.
🌞 Sunlight
Give your plants bright, indirect light. Avoid harsh afternoon sun unless the plant loves full sun.
🍃 Natural Boosters
Use diluted Aloe Vera water or mix Vitamin E capsules in soil occasionally for growth.
🌬️ Let Them Breathe
Don’t keep adding fertilizers or sprays daily. Allow your plants to adapt and grow naturally.
🌿 Turmeric Magic
Adding a pinch of turmeric to soil helps prevent fungal infections and keeps roots healthy.
🍌 Banana Water
Water your plants with banana peel water once every 1–2 weeks for a natural potassium boost.
🪴 Soil Digging
Loosen the top layer of soil every few weeks. It improves air circulation and water absorption for roots.

Common Problems 🐛

🐛 Insects & Caterpillars
Look for chewed leaves or tiny pests. Remove by hand or spray with mild neem water.
🥚 Moth Eggs
Check the underside of leaves for tiny red/yellow eggs. Gently wipe and destroy before they hatch.
🐌 Snails & Slugs
Common during monsoon. Remove carefully by hand in the evening or set natural traps.
🍂 Dry or Infected Leaves
Regularly prune dry, yellow, or diseased leaves to help the plant focus on healthy growth.
💦 Overwatering
Too much water causes root rot. Always check soil moisture before watering.
☀️ Sunburn
Brown patches on leaves mean too much direct sunlight. Move to indirect or shaded light.
